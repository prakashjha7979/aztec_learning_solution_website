Why to join Aztec?

Aztec Learning Solutions offers a great opportunity to recent graduates from any stream,who are looking for an exciting career in the thriving IT & KPO sectors in collaborations with a IREF(Institute of Real Estate and Finance).

What we actually offer?

We offer a IT and Non-IT Job oriented courses in collaboration with Institute Of Real Estate And Finance(IREF),Pune for Undergraduate and Graduated students.For Undergraduate we offer them Advance Diploma certificate Programme along with guaranteed Job placement and for Graduate students we offer them Master Program certificate which is also Job guaranteed Placement.

What's the chances of getting placed through this Programme?

This Programme is comes with Guaranteed Job Placements for freshers and Professionals.We have dedicated team to ensure you get your dream job with 100% JOB Guarantee.
